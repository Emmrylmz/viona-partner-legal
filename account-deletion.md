# Account Deletion Request - Viona Partner

**Last Updated: January 4, 2025**

## How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your Viona Partner account using one of the following methods:

### Option 1: In-App Deletion

1. Open the Viona Partner app
2. Go to **Account** > **Preferences**
3. Select **Delete Account**
4. Confirm your decision

### Option 2: Email Request

Send a deletion request to **support@viona.app** with:

- Subject: "Account Deletion Request - Viona Partner"
- Your registered email address
- Your venue name (for verification)

## What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, we will remove:

- Your profile information (name, email, phone number)
- Your venue information and settings
- Service offerings and pricing data
- Schedule and availability settings
- Staff member associations
- Push notification tokens and device information
- App preferences and settings

## What Data May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

- **Transaction records** - Required for tax and accounting compliance
- **Reservation history** - May be retained in anonymized form for dispute resolution
- **Legal compliance data** - As required by applicable regulations
- **Anonymized analytics** - For service improvement (not linked to your identity)

## Important Considerations

### Staff Accounts

If you are a venue owner:

- Deleting your account will remove all associated staff access
- Staff members will be notified of the account deletion
- Staff accounts linked only to your venue will be deactivated

If you are a staff member:

- Deleting your account will remove your access to all associated venues
- The venue owner will be notified
- Your activities and data within the venue will be disassociated from your identity

### Customer Impact

- Existing reservations will remain visible to customers
- Your venue will no longer appear for new bookings
- Customers will be notified if they have upcoming reservations

### Data Export

Before deleting your account, you may request an export of your data by contacting support@viona.app.

## Processing Time

- **In-App Deletion**: Processed immediately
- **Email Requests**: Processed within 7 business days

## Permanent Action

Account deletion is **permanent and cannot be undone**. If you wish to use Viona Partner again after deletion, you will need to create a new account.
